Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mariposa?
Mariposa is the world's most functional and decentralized metaverse platform built on the Binance blockchain. Users can produce virtual reality content on the platform, experience the virtual world and earn income from the content they produce.
What are NFT’s?
NFT (Non-fungible Tokens) are secure, digital certificates that prove ownership of unique items. These items can be digital assets such as art, e-books and animations. All digital assets can now be given ownership rights by associating an NFT, which means that they can be bought, sold, auctioned or gifted just like physical items. NFTs can only have one official owner at a time. They are written into a public blockchain database so it’s transparent as to who owns them, who first made them and what they have sold for. NFTs make digital ownership permanent and beyond the lifetime of any company or platform.
How are NFT’s made?
NFTs are made in the same way that cryptocurrency tokens are generated - they are written into the code of a secure, public database called a blockchain. This process is known as minting.
How much does it cost to mint NFT’s on Mariposa?
It costs less than a dollar on Mariposa to mint an NFT. That’s because we mint on a blockchain called Binance Smart Chain. This blockchain uses a Proof of Stake (PoS) consensus to mint NFTs. It offers quick transactions with very low minting costs. In contrast, minting on other platforms like Opensea can cost up to $200. Opensea uses another blockchain called Ethereum which has high transaction costs ("gas fees").
